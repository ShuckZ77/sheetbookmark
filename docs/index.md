# Bookmark Sheet Sync

**Every bookmark, from every browser, in one Google Sheet you own.**

Star a page in Chrome and it lands in the `Chrome` tab of a spreadsheet in your own Google Drive —
within seconds. Firefox writes to its own tab, Edge to its own. Search and open all of them from
any browser's popup, or pull your other browsers' bookmarks into this one with a click.

## Why people like it

- **Your data stays yours.** The sheet lives in *your* Drive. There is no server, no account, no
  tracking — the extension cannot even see other files in your Drive (Google's `drive.file` scope
  enforces this, we don't just promise it).
- **A tab per browser**, so nothing overlaps or gets overwritten — rename the sheet or its tabs
  freely, sync follows.
- **Zero-friction setup**: install, click *Connect Google Sheets*, done. Existing bookmarks are
  imported automatically.
- **Your cadence**: instant, every 15 min / hour / 8 h / day, or manual with a pending-count badge.
- **Notes column**: toolbar saves auto-fill the page description; edit or add your own notes in the
  sheet — they're never overwritten.
